A New Olympic Sport: Flag Football [B1]

È un momento emozionante per il flag football, sport che si sta facendo conoscere in tutto il mondo e che nel 2028 farà il suo debutto alle Olimpiadi.

American football is known for causing debilitating head injuries, mostly from players tackling each other — an unfortunate trend that inspired the 2015 biographical sports drama Concussion, starring Will Smith. Perhaps this is why the non-contact version of American football, flag football, has become so popular in recent years. Growing in popularity worldwide, flag football is to make its debut at the next Olympics, which take place in Los Angeles, California in the summer of 2028.

A LOT LESS CONTACT

In flag football, just like in American football, two teams compete to score points by moving the ball down the field, into the other team’s end zone. However, unlike in American football, in flag football the players wear flags on their waist and instead of tackling each other, they try to pull the flag of the player with the ball to stop them. So while American football is a high-impact contact sport, the contact between players in flag football is very limited, and so the risk of injury is significantly reduced.

WORLD WAR TWO

Flag football was originally played on US military bases during World War Two. Originally known as touch and tail football, it began as a recreational sport for army personnel as a means to remain active while not risking the injuries associated with American football. The first known game of flag football was at Fort Meade in Maryland, which is now considered the birthplace of the sport.

GLOBAL REACH

After the war, flag football continued to be played on US military bases and soon caught on among civilians living across the US. The National Flag Football Association was formed in 1971 to govern the sport in the US. And the International Federation of American Football (IFAF) oversees the sport internationally since 2002. 

OLYMPIC SPORT

According to the IFAF, flag football is played by more than twenty million people in more than one hundred countries and will soon become more popular than American football. The sport debuted at the 2022 World Games, an international multi-sport competition including non-Olympic sports, and will be played at this summer’s World Games in Chengdu, China. Both male and female teams will compete for the first time at the 2028 Olympics and flag football will be introduced to millions more sports fans, contributing to its popularity.
